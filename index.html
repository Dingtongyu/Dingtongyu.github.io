Project Summary

Introduction:

Relevant research and analysis on MOSFET(METAL-OXIDE-SEMICONDUCTOR FIELD-EFFECT TRANSISTOR), based on fundamental function and structure of MOSFET. Then build p-type MOSFET in COMSOL MULTIPHYSICS and do relevant analysis.

Experimental target:

(1)learn basic knowledge of semiconductor

（a）Electron bands

（b）Hole bands

（c）Function of Doping

   (d) Characteristics of diode

(2)understand function and structure of MOSFET

(3)build the model of MOSFET and analyze

Methods:

(1)Application of two software(COMSOL and MATLAB)

(2)Searching unknown relevant knowledge on the Internet

(3)Learning to ask “Why” , brainstorming independently and finding the solution to the problems.

As can be seen from the above graph, p-type area has higher hole concentration, whereas, n-type area has low hole concentration.

Then I focused on researching the electron concentration below gate with different voltage across the gate. So, firstly, V-I graph could be gained from this semiconductor.

As can be inferred from the above graph, this MOSFET could allow the flow of current between the voltage -3V and -2V

In the range of voltage(-1V~1V), It could be observed the electron concentration below the gate with different voltage.

When V_G = -1V
It could be observed clearly that it had no channel between two p-type areas below the gate, so it had no current.

When voltage=-2V,it could be observed that it has a thin channel which has high hole concentration,this MOSFET could have electricity under this voltage.
